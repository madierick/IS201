    #1 Stress Reliever
    • Running can boost your feel-good endorphins and distract you from daily worries
    • Increases concentrations of norepinephrine
    #2 Increased Energy
    • The strenuous exercise increases your rate of metabolism
    • Increases production of vital hormones
    #3 Increases Mental Capacity
    • Exercise and running can produce new brain cells in certain locations through a process called neurogenesis
    • Increases levels of a brain-derived protein in the body
